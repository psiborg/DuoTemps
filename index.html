📊 About Feels-Like Temperature

Wind Chill: Applies when temperature is ≤10°C and wind speed is ≥5 km/h. Uses Environment Canada formula to calculate heat loss from exposed skin.

Heat Index: Applies when temperature is between 27°C and 43°C (80-110°F). Uses NOAA Rothfusz regression to calculate apparent temperature based on humidity levels.

Feels Like: Shows actual temperature when conditions don't meet thresholds for Wind Chill or Heat Index.
